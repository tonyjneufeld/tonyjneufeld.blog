<div class="services-faq mt-5 mb-4">
  <h2>Common Questions</h2>

  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Do I need to share all my financial details upfront?</summary>
    <p>Nope. The discovery call is just a conversation. We'll figure out what you're comfortable sharing as we go. You're always in control.</p>
  </details>

  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Are you a financial advisor?</summary>
    <p>No. I don't sell products, earn commissions, or manage your investments. I'm a coach and educator who helps you build the habits, systems, and clarity to make your own confident financial decisions.</p>
  </details>

  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>What if I'm not sure which tier is right for me?</summary>
    <p>That's exactly what the free call is for. Most people start with The Check-Up and go from there. There's no pressure to commit to more than you need.</p>
  </details>

  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Can I switch between tiers later?</summary>
    <p>Absolutely. Your needs will change, and so can your plan. Many people start with The Game Plan and move into The Co-Pilot once they have momentum.</p>
  </details>

  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Is this only for people in Saskatchewan?</summary>
    <p>I'm based in Saskatoon, and would love to meet locally with people who are in town. I will work with Canadians across the country virtually.</p>
  </details>

  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>What's the cancellation policy for The Co-Pilot?</summary>
    <p>Cancel anytime. No contracts, no penalties. If it's not working, I'd rather you tell me than stick around out of obligation.</p>
  </details>

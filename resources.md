<h2 class="mt-5 mb-4">Recommended Books</h2>

<p>These are the books that influenced my approach to money, work, and life. And before you go and buy any of them, go get them for free from your local library!</p>

<div class="row">
  <div class="col-md-4 mb-4">
    <div class="book-card">
      <h3>Fiction</h3>
      <ul>
        <li><em>And Then There Were None</em> by Agatha Christie — A quick read, and one of my favourite mysteries.</li>
        <li><em>Lord of the Flies</em> by William Golding — Another quick read, but an absolute classic.</li>
        <li><em>1984</em> by George Orwell — Incredibly relevant for today's day and age.</li>
        <li><em>The Shining</em> by Stephen King — I didn't sleep for a couple weeks after this one.</li>
        <li><em>The Way of Kings</em> by Brandon Sanderson — An incredible journey, this book is long, but completely on another level.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-4 mb-4">
    <div class="book-card">
      <h3>Financial Independence &amp; Money</h3>
      <ul>
        <li><em>5 Years to Freedom</em> by Rejean Venne — This Canadian-based guide will help you retire way earlier than you think possible.</li>
        <li><em>Your Money or Your Life</em> by Vicki Robin — A mindset reset on how we exchange life energy for money.</li>
        <li><em>The Psychology of Money</em> by Morgan Housel — A fantastic bundle of stories about wealth, greed, and happiness.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
  <div class="col-md-4 mb-4">
    <div class="book-card">
      <h3>Lifestyle Design &amp; Work</h3>
      <ul>
        <li><em>Deep Work</em> by Cal Newport — Essential reading on cultivating focus and doing meaningful work.</li>
        <li><em>Digital Minimalism</em> by Cal Newport — Helps you cut digital clutter and reclaim attention.</li>
        <li><em>Atomic Habits</em> by James Clear — Practical strategies for building habits that stick.</li>
        <li><em>Four Thousand Weeks</em> by Oliver Burkeman — A thoughtful take on time management and living intentionally.</li>
        <li><em>The 4-Hour Workweek</em> by Tim Ferriss — A bold playbook for designing work and lifestyle on your own terms.</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

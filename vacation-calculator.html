Assumptions & tips
  • Driving distances are approximate from city centre to city centre.
  • Flight costs are rough round-trip estimates — check Google Flights for real prices.
  • Accommodation rates vary hugely by season, booking lead time, and location. These are starting estimates.
  • All-inclusive rates include food and drinks — the calculator zeros out your food cost when you pick that option.
  • This calculator uses Canadian dollars (CAD).
  • Travel insurance is not included — budget an extra $50–$150/person for international trips.
  • Pro tip: Track the real cost of your next vacation and compare it to this estimate. That's how you build a travel budget you can trust.

💡 Tip: Change the travel mode between Drive and Fly to instantly compare costs for the same trip.
